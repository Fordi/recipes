# Guga's Korean Corn Dogs

## Dry

Combine:

* 400g of bread flour
* 10g of salt

# Wet

* 300 ml of warm water
* 60g of sugar
* Sprinkle 8g of yeast

2. Knead five minutes
3. Let double in size in a greased glass bowl
