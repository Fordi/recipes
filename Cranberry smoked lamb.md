Cranberry smoked lamb
---------------------

* Hunk of lamb
* 1 1/2 tbsp rosemary
* 1/2 tbsp sausage seasoning
* 1 tsp raw sugar
* 1/4 cup craisins

2. Coat meat with craisin crust

5. When smoker reaches 500, sear meat for no more than 1 minute per side
